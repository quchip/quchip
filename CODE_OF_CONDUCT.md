# Code of Conduct

*Fear the old blood. Fear more an unkind hunter.*

**I. ARISE NOW, YE TARNISHED.** *(Elden Ring)* Ye dead who yet live, ye contributors of no renown — all are welcome. The guidance of grace ([CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md)) will show thee the way.

**II. HESITATION IS DEFEAT.** *(Sekiro, Isshin)* Ask thy question plainly and at once. None shall be scorned for not yet knowing the way.

**III. A HUNTER MUST HUNT.** *(Bloodborne, Gehrman)* Bugs, and only bugs. Never fellow hunters. Harassment and attacks upon the person end the hunt at once.

**IV. GRANT US EYES.** *(Bloodborne, Micolash)* Review is insight freely given. "Git gud" grants none. Explain. Cite. Suggest.

**V. SHADOWS DIE TWICE.** *(Sekiro)* A rejected pull request is not death. Revise, and return by the Sculptor's idol.

**VI. BEWARE OF PATCHES.** *(he appeareth in every game)* In all lands the trusty one waiteth, promising treasure past yonder ledge. The untested patch kicketh thee into the pit.

**VII. "PUT THESE FOOLISH AMBITIONS TO REST."** *(Elden Ring, Margit)* Spoken only of the colossal unbidden rewrite — never of an honest question. Open an issue first; small PRs kindle brightest.

Stray, and thou shalt be recalled to a site of grace bearing a warning. Persist, and the fog wall closeth before thee — banished, runeless, maidenless forevermore.

*In plain modern words: be kind, critique code not people, no harassment. Report via GitHub issue, or privately to the maintainers at conduct@quchip.org. Maintainers may warn, then ban.*

Farewell, good hunter. May you find your merge, in the waking world. \\[T]/
